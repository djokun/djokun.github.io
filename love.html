I Love You, Taylor!



The winds of chance brought us together
and proved that although I thought
I'd be alone forever
I'm moved to the brink of tears.
I'm so happy because I think
I've found my one true love.

My sweet Taylor,
I love thee
as a sailor
loves the sea.
Inseparable, in clear skies
or rough waters.
Between us lies
the only thing that matters.

Never alone, never again.
Together or apart
no matter what
your hand will forever hold my heart.
